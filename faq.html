Got Questions?

We Have Answers

Find quick answers to the most common questions about staying at Debbie's Suites & Apartments. Can't find what you need? Contact us directly.

How do I make a reservation at Debbie's Suites?
You can book directly on our website via the Book Now page. Fill in your details and our team will confirm your reservation within 24 hours. You can also call us on 08091766665 or 09033931121, or email us at debbiesupperclass@gmail.com.
Is online payment required to confirm a booking?
No online payment is required at the booking stage. Simply submit your reservation request and our team will reach out to confirm availability and discuss payment arrangements. Payment can be made on arrival or via bank transfer as agreed.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2–3 days in advance to guarantee your preferred room type. During peak periods such as public holidays, festive seasons and weekends, we strongly advise booking 1–2 weeks ahead. For event hall bookings, contact us as early as possible to secure your preferred date.
Can I modify or cancel my booking?
Yes. You can modify or cancel your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your check-in date. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before arrival may incur a fee. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make any changes.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Standard check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 Noon (Midday). Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and may attract an additional fee. Please contact our reception team in advance.
What ID documents do I need at check-in?
All guests are required to present a valid government-issued photo ID at check-in. Accepted documents include National ID Card, International Passport, Driver's Licence or Voter's Card. For corporate guests, an official company ID or letter may also be accepted.
What amenities are included in the rooms?
All our rooms include: free high-speed WiFi, smart flat-screen TV, air conditioning, en-suite bathroom, fully equipped kitchen (or mini kitchen in standard rooms), daily housekeeping, and 24-hour room service. Executive suites and apartments include additional premium features such as separate living areas, dining spaces and larger kitchens.
Is WiFi really free for all guests?
Yes! High-speed WiFi is completely free and available throughout the entire property — in all guest rooms, public areas, the restaurant, lounge and pool area. Simply ask reception for the current WiFi credentials.
Do all rooms have kitchens?
Yes. All our rooms and suites come with cooking facilities. Standard rooms include a mini kitchen with a cooker and basic utensils. Executive suites and apartment suites feature full kitchens with a cooker, refrigerator, microwave, pots, pans and crockery — perfect for longer stays or family meals.
What types of events can be hosted at Debbie's Upperclass?
Our event centre is fully equipped to host a wide range of occasions including weddings, receptions, engagement parties, birthday celebrations, corporate events, conferences, seminars, product launches, graduations and any other special celebrations. Our team works with you to tailor every detail.
How do I book the event hall?
To enquire about event hall availability and packages, please call us on 08091766665, email us or fill in our contact form. We recommend reaching out well in advance for popular dates.
What cuisines do the restaurants serve?
We have two restaurants: one specialising in authentic Nigerian cuisine — including jollof rice, egusi soup, pepper soup, suya and more — and a second serving continental cuisine featuring international dishes, grilled meals, pasta, salads and a variety of other global favourites.
What are the restaurant opening hours?
Our restaurants are open daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Room service is available 24 hours for in-room dining. The pool bar operates from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily.
Is the swimming pool free for hotel guests?
Yes! Both the adult swimming pool and children's pool are available to all hotel guests at no additional charge. The pools are open from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Towels are available at the poolside.
Is the gym available to all guests?
Yes, our fully equipped fitness centre is available to all hotel guests. The gym is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Day-use access for non-hotel guests may be arranged — please enquire at reception.
Is smoking allowed on the property?
Debbie's Suites & Apartments is a non-smoking property. Smoking is strictly prohibited in all indoor areas including rooms, restaurants, gym and event halls. Designated outdoor smoking areas are available. A cleaning fee may apply to rooms where smoking violations occur.
Are pets allowed?
Unfortunately, we do not currently accommodate pets in our guest rooms or public areas. We apologise for any inconvenience and hope to introduce a pet-friendly policy in the future.
Is there parking available?
Yes! We offer free, secure and spacious on-site parking for all guests. Our premises are gated with 24-hour security and CCTV surveillance to ensure your vehicle is safe throughout your stay.

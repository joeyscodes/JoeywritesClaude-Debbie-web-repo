Who We Are

Built on a Vision
of True Excellence

Debbie's Suites & Apartments was born from a singular vision — to create a world-class hospitality destination that reflects the warmth, richness and pride of Nigerian culture, while meeting the highest international standards of luxury and comfort.

Strategically located at the scenic Abuja–Nasarawa border in Orozo, FCT, we offer guests an escape from the bustle of the city without sacrificing access to its conveniences. Our lush green surroundings, mountain backdrop and signature green building have become a beloved landmark in the region.

Our Vision

To be West Africa's most celebrated boutique luxury hotel, event and recreation destination — where every guest feels like royalty.

Our Mission

To deliver consistently exceptional hospitality experiences through premium facilities, attentive service and genuine Nigerian warmth.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Warmth, Innovation and Community. These principles guide every decision we make and every experience we create.

Our Facilities

A Complete Lifestyle Destination

Everything you need for an extraordinary stay — all in one address.

50 Guest Rooms

From comfortable standard rooms to spacious multi-bedroom apartment suites, all fully furnished with premium amenities and smart appliances.

Premium Event Centre

State-of-the-art halls for weddings, corporate events, conferences, seminars and all celebrations — tailored to every occasion.

Two Restaurants

Savour authentic Nigerian delicacies and international continental cuisine prepared by our skilled culinary team in two dedicated restaurants.

Swimming Pools

Unwind in our pristine adult swimming pool or let the little ones enjoy the dedicated children's pool — refreshing fun for all ages.

Fitness Centre

A fully equipped professional gym with modern cardio and strength training equipment to keep you at your best while away from home.

Lounge & Bar

Our stylish indoor lounge, breezy outdoor terrace and poolside bar offer the perfect atmosphere for relaxation, socialising and celebration.

Getting Here

By Car

Head along Nyanya-Karu-Karshi Road from Nyanya. Continue past Karu until you reach KM 7 marker at Orozo. We are visible on your right with our distinctive green building.

By Public Transport

Take a bus or shared taxi heading towards Karshi from Nyanya or Karu junction. Alight at the KM 7 Orozo stop and look for the Debbie's Upperclass sign.

Need Directions?

Don't hesitate to call us. Our friendly reception team will guide you every step of the way. We can also arrange a pickup from major Abuja landmarks.

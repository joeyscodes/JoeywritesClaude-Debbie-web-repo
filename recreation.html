Leisure Spaces

Outdoor & Lounge Areas

Pool Bar

Sip on freshly crafted cocktails, tropical juices and premium drinks right at the water's edge. Our pool bar is open daily from 10 AM to 9 PM — the perfect way to elevate your pool day.

Outdoor Lounge

Unwind on our beautifully landscaped outdoor terrace with comfortable loungers, ambient lighting and a serene atmosphere. Perfect for evening relaxation with a drink in hand.

Indoor Lounge

Our stylish, air-conditioned indoor lounge is a sophisticated retreat for socialising, casual meetings or simply relaxing after a long day. Premium bar service is available throughout.

Landscaped Gardens

Stroll through our beautifully maintained grounds with lush greenery, potted palms and ornamental plants that create a peaceful, resort-like atmosphere in every corner.

Secure Grounds

Our fully gated and landscaped compound with ample secure parking offers guests total peace of mind. CCTV surveillance and on-site security operate 24 hours a day.

Connected Throughout

Stay connected everywhere on the property. Free high-speed WiFi is available in all outdoor and indoor leisure areas — by the pool, in the gym and throughout the lounge.
